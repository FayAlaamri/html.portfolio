The Best Movies According to Fay

My top 3 Movies of all-time.

HOWL'S MOVING CASTEL

I have great admiration for this movie as it portrays a deep and captivating love story.

ARRIETTY

I love this movie because it shows Arrietty as a strong, independent lady.
It also highlights that size doesn't matter and that we can be friends despite our differences.

PONYO

Ponyo holds a special place in my heart because I watched it with my closest friends and we all enjoyed it.
